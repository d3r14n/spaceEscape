Space Escape is a game where you need to advance the most you can before getting hit.

This is you, move yourself with the arrow keys or the keys W,S,A and D.
This is where you're gonna shot, move the mouse to change position,
click to shot, you can only shot in front of you.
These are the enemy ships, destroy as many as you can while avoiding their bullets. Don't crash with them either, that will also destroy your ship.
When you destroy an enemy ship, they will explode, their explosions doesn't harm you, but your bullets aren't going to pass through.
